Who We Are

Transform Your Outdoor Space with Dublin and Kildare's Premiere Gardening and Landscaping Service

11+ Years of Experience
in Landscaping.

At MJ Gardening & Landscapes, we believe every season offers an opportunity to enhance your outdoor living. From meticulous garden care to stunning landscape transformations, our expert team delivers first-class gardening services across Dublin and Kildare.

Based in Leixlip, Kildare, we're dedicated to bringing excellence to gardens across Dublin and Kildare. Our commitment to the highest standards of workmanship, combined with our reasonable rates, makes us the go-to choice for all your gardening and landscaping needs.

Garden Maintenance

Our comprehensive garden care services include mowing, Hedgetrimming, Weeding, and Ivy Removal

Tree Surgery

Covering everything from pruning to full removals. Prioritizing the health and safety of your trees, for optimal growth.

Turf and Artificial Grass

Whether you prefer low-maintenance artificial grass or instant natural turf. Both options provide a lush look year-round,

Paving

Using various materials, from traditional brick to contemporary porcelain, we create beautiful, functional spaces.

Fencing

Powerwashing

Exterior Painting

Composite Decking

This modern alternative to wood offers the warmth and appeal of timber without the maintenance.
